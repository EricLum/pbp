Tickets are now available for purchase.

Plate by Plate, Project by Project NY's signature annual tasting benefit, is back this year at Metropolitan Pavilion! We will be featuring many of New York's finest restaurant and beverage purveyors, all for a great cause!
At Plate by Plate NYC 2018, you'll get to enjoy quality food, engage in stimulating conversation, participate in our silent auction, and receive free entry to our after party to dance the night away. It is THE ultimate foodie charity event where 100% of the net ticket proceeds raised will be remitted to our beneficiary partner this year, Coalition for Asian American Children + Families (CACF)!
About our beneficiary partner: CACF is the nation's only pan-Asian children and families' advocacy organization, bringing together community-based organizations (CBOs) to fight for equity for the Asian Pacific American community. The coalition’s work is especially compelling because they train and equip community members, including students, parents, teachers, and CBO staff, to become forward-thinking change-makers, and to push for change in such places as schools and workplaces. We are excited to work with CACF and support their mission as part of our 2018 theme of Asian Americans and Immigration!
 
We look forward to your attendance at Plate by Plate to support us in continuing our efforts to serve the Asian American community and work with Asian American non-profits in need.
